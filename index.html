CAN Protocol & Interview Questions

CAN Transport Protocol (ISO 15765-2)

  • CAN TP frame types: SF (Single Frame), FF (First Frame), CF (Consecutive Frame), FC (Flow Control)
  • Segmentation and reassembly of long messages
  • Flow control mechanisms and timeout management
  • Parameters: BS (Block Size), STmin (Separation Time)
